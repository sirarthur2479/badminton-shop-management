Stringing Kiosk — Overview

The iPad on the counter is a self-service kiosk. When you drop off a racket for restringing, you register the order yourself — no waiting for staff, no paper note to lose.

The form takes about 60 seconds. You'll choose your racket, string, tension, and leave your contact details so the shop can notify you when it's ready.

Your racket, your specs You control exactly what string and tension goes in your racket — there's no back-and-forth. If you're not sure what tension to pick, check the recommended range shown next to each string model.

Step by Step

1

Select your racket

Tap the Racket Brand dropdown and pick your brand (Yonex, Victor, Li-Ning, Babolat, or Carlton). Then pick your model from the list that appears. If your model isn't listed, mention it in the Notes field at the end.

2

Choose your string

Select the String Brand and then the String Model. The model list shows the recommended tension range — e.g. BG80 is rated 19–30 lbs. Pick whatever suits your playing level and frame.

3

Set the tension

Enter your preferred tension in pounds. The field won't accept a value outside the string's rated range — if it turns red, adjust up or down. Club players typically string 24–26 lbs; competitive players 28–30 lbs.

4

Enter your contact details

Your name is required. Add a phone number, email, or both — at least one contact method is needed so the shop can reach you when the racket is done. If you add an email, you'll get an automatic notification.

5

Add any notes (optional)

Use the Notes field for anything special: "handle has a crack near grip end", "please use the same tension as last time", "racket belongs to my daughter". The stringer reads this before starting.

6

Tap Submit

Your order is registered instantly. The confirmation screen shows your Order ID — screenshot it or write it down. You'll use this number to identify your racket at pickup.

Your Order ID

After submitting, you'll see a confirmation screen with a unique order ID. Keep this — it's how the shop tracks your racket in the system.

When you return to pick up your racket, give the order ID or your name and the shop can pull up the record immediately.

No printer on the kiosk The kiosk doesn't print a receipt. Screenshot or photograph the confirmation screen before you leave, or the shop will look you up by name.

Browse the Online Shop

The shop has a product page you can browse from your phone or any browser. It shows in-stock products with photos, prices, and any current sale items.

Category tabs

Products are organised into tabs: Racket, String, Grip, Shuttle, Bag, and Shoe. Tap a tab to filter by type, or stay on "All" to browse everything.

Search

Type in the search box at the top to find a specific product by name. Results filter as you type — search "Yonex" to see all Yonex products, or "BG80" to find that specific string.

Sale items

Products on sale show a Sale badge with the original price crossed out and the sale price highlighted. The "On Sale" tab shows only discounted items.

Send an Inquiry

If you're interested in one or more products — to check availability, ask about sizing, or get a price — you can send an inquiry directly from the shop page. It works like a shopping cart but instead of checking out, you're asking the shop a question.

1

Add items to your inquiry

Tap "Add to Inquiry" on any product card. A badge appears in the top corner counting how many items you've added. Keep browsing and adding — you can include as many items as you like.

2

Open the inquiry sheet

Tap the badge (or the cart icon) to open the inquiry panel. You'll see all your selected items with quantities. Tap + or − to adjust quantities, or the X to remove an item.

3

Add your message and contact

Write a short message — "Do you have the Astrox 99 in the lighter version?" or "What's the best string for a beginner?". Add your name and at least one contact method (phone or email).

4

Send

Tap Send Inquiry. The shop receives it immediately with your full item list and message. You'll get an inquiry ID on the confirmation screen — keep it for reference.

The shop will reply to you directly Inquiry responses come via phone or email — whichever contact you provided. The shop typically replies within the same business day.

FAQ

My racket model isn't in the list. What do I do?
Select the closest brand, pick any model, and describe your actual racket in the Notes field. The stringer will see it and sort it out.
I'm not sure what tension to use.
A general guide: beginners and casual players 22–24 lbs, club players 24–27 lbs, advanced/competitive players 27–30 lbs. When in doubt, go 24–25 lbs — it's forgiving and suits most recreational play.
I didn't get a confirmation email.
Check your spam folder. If it's not there, the order still went through — your order ID on the confirmation screen is the definitive record. You can also call the shop and quote your name.
How long will my racket take?
Turnaround time depends on the shop's queue. You'll get an email when it's done. If you need it by a specific date, add that to the Notes field.
Can I change my order after submitting?
Contact the shop directly once you have your order ID — they can update the details if stringing hasn't started yet.
My items disappeared from the inquiry cart.
The inquiry cart is saved in your browser. Closing the browser tab clears it. If this happens, simply add your items again — it only takes a moment.
